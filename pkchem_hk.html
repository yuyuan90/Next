ABOUT US

Who We Are

PKchem(HK) is setting up and operating under the laws of the people’s republic of China. It is an ISO9001 certificated company and can provide professional services in laboratory analysis area. There is a talented staff team each with high academic qualification, outstanding professional knowledge and solid theoretical basis, 60% of which are Ph.D or master degree holders and the rest are graduates from domestic well-known universities.

What We Do

We have a professional team to provide Laboratory Integrated Solution. We provide: Laboratory furniture and ventilation equipment; laboratory analysis proposal; establishment of analytic method, equipment model selection/ procurement/ installation/ commissioning/ training; establishment of laboratory management systems. An optimized laboratory design proposal will not only save costs in construction & operation, but also improve the efficiency and accuracy in laboratory analysis. PKchem(HK) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of PKchem. It was established to be fully responsible for the operation of the general contracting project.

Our Mission

- To provide our customers with the besst solution based on udnerstaning our customers' needs and requirements.

- To enable our customers to utilize their systems to their full potential to benefit them in their work.

- To exceed our customers' expectation with first class service and support.
